About Me


Who is Corey Maxedon

I am a first year Master of Statistics (Class of 2021) student at Indiana University. I graduated in May of 2019 from Indiana State University with a Bachelor of Science in Finance with a concentration in Financial Analysis and Investment Management and a minor in Mathematics. I also graduated the Honors College at ISU with Summa Cum Laude Greek Honors.

The field of finance has always interested me. Mathematics has been one of my strengths throughout school. I also have a need to help people which is seen in my experience as a Certified Nursing Assistant and my numerous volunteer opportunities. Statistics with a finance background meets my interests perfectly by combining math skills with helping the world through data analysis.

My main goal is to learn more about the role statistics plays in the financial industry as well as the medical field and government. Because of this, I am actively searching for internship or job shadowing opportunities for the 2019-2020 school year. If you have any questions or professional advice, I am always open to email at corey@maxedon.net or connect with me using the contact form below. Don't forget to check out my resume!
